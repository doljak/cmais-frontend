Series 1

Rose Tyler meets a mysterious stranger called The Doctor, and her life will never be the same again.

Series 2

Together, the Doctor and Rose travel back in time to the Highlands of Scotland, to find Queen Victoria confronted by a terrifying Werewolf; and back further still to 18th Century France, where lethal Clockwork Androids stalk Versailles. In the future, an evil race of Cat Nurses hide a sinister secret at the heart of their hospital – and the human race is threatened by a remorseless silver army of emotionless Cybermen.

Series 3

From the moment the Doctor walks into the life of medical student Martha Jones he changes it forever. Together they find themselves travelling through time and space encountering extraordinary events, amazing worlds and faces both old and new.
