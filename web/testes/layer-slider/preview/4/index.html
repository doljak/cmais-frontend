Responsive Layer Slider comes with tones of features!

Bellow you can find a list of just a few of them, make sure to check them out.

Responsive

This slider is fully responsive, it was also optimized for mobile devices in terms of performance and usability. Get the same experience on all your devices.

Rapid Setup

All of the preview versions of the slider are included in the download package. If you want to save time you can always build on top of that, it is easy!

Made with Love

In MassivePixelCreation we have a passion to make great things. Thats why our products are unique and original in every way. We also focus on performance and usability.

Easy Customization

There are tones of customization options, don’t get scared it comes with an extensive documentation and support forums. It fits every needs.

9 Different UI Styles

You can choose between 9 different UI styles all you need to do is change a class for the UI element.

3 Shadow Styles

You can choose between 3 different shadow styles all you need to do is add a shadow class to the slider element.

Almost 200 Animation Effects

You can choose between 6 different animation effects and 32 easing types which gives almost 200 animation possibilities.

Video API Support

You can choose between Vimeo or YoutTube videos, the slideshow will pause if the video is playing. Also you can auto play the video when the slide has changed.

Touch Enabled

There is no need for UI elements on mobile devices thats why Responsive Layer Slider is touch enabled.

SEO Friendly

The slider is SEO friendly, all of its content is easily readable to search engines.
